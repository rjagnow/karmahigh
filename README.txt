Karma High

An 8-bit game of students navigating the halls of Karma High School.
The game is designed to encourage empathy and a discussion around bullying.

Started Dec. 3, 2011 as part of the AngelHack Hack-a-Thon in San Francisco.
____

Conventions:
Horizontal scrolling hallway with 1280x480 pixel backgrounds.
640x480 game window with pixel-doubling for all artwork.
40x80 characters with a 40x40 collision region at their base.
